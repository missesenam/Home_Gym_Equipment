In helping to develop and energizing muscle growth dumbbells activate a number of different muscles. a dumbbell is a weight training type of exercise equipment. it is also called hand weights and free weights. they come in handy for exercising without machines. exercise ball

Exercise balls, also known as physio balls, Swiss balls, and fit balls, are large, vinyl balls used for exercise. They help to strengthen the muscles in your abdomen and back, as well as improve core stability and balance. They help to reduce muscle stiffness, fatigue, and improve muscle strength.

Kettlebell

A kettlebell is a cast-iron or cast-steel ball with a handle attached to the top used in weight training. It is used for a variety of exercises, including ballistic exercises that incorporate cardiovascular, strength, and flexibility training.
